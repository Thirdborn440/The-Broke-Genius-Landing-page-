About the Book

The Broke Genius offers a blueprint for financial success, drawing from the experiences of well-known entrepreneurs like Bill Gates, Elon Musk, Warren Buffett, and others. This book is designed to help you unlock the potential to overcome your financial obstacles, succeed in business, and live a life of purpose.
